Free Crate is a free music curation/distribution service that recreates the experience of vinyl crate-digging, and of falling in love with new music, for the internet landscape. We make it easier to find great music you haven't yet heard by scouring the internet and working with new emerging talent to give you a meticulously curated "crate" of the 25 best tracks released for free each Friday.

All you have to do is download and discover.
